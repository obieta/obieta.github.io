Instructions

Problem statement

Pose this problem. A factory line conveyor belt passes through a robot station equipped with a robotic hand and vision recognition system to sort random children's building blocks on the belt into bins.

Widgets may be small, triangles, rounds, squares, black, large, white, triangles, or appropriate combinations thereof. Each bin should be:
  1. Assigned maximum four small objects.
  2. Assigned maximum seven large objects.
  3. A general shape may have no more than four instances of a specific color.
  4. A general shape may have no more than five instances of a single size.
How would we test such a system?
Kids need a variety of building blocks!
